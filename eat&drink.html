Restaurants:

Dublin is a mix of everything, and you can taste that mix, here are some restaurants suggestions: Ummi Falafel, Cornucopia, Manifesto, The Ivy, Thunder Road Café, Wagamama, Elephant & Castle, Sophie's @ The Dean, Mad Egg, Angelina's.

Gastro Pub:

The best part of going to the pub is that usually you can enjoy a nice meal too, here are some suggestions: Bow Lane, Napper Tandy’s, The Exchequer, The Stag's Head, The Legal Eagle, The Porterhouse, Peruke & Periwig, The Camden Exchange.

Cafés(coffe and brunch):

To enjoy a nice morning or to heal from a hangover, Dublin offer the best Cafés to you: Shoe Lane, Indigo, Bother Hubbard, Thundercut Alley, Two Boys Brew, Meet me in the Morning, Two Pup's Coffe, The White Moose Café, WUFF, The Fumbally.
